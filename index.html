Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect
When you subscribe to alerts or our newsletter, we collect your email address only. When you sign the petition, we collect your name and email. We do not collect your location, device data, or browsing behaviour.
How we use your data
Email addresses are used only to send layoff alerts, newsletter digests, or petition updates that you explicitly signed up for. We never use your email for any other purpose.
We never sell your data
Your email address is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for commercial purposes.
Affiliate links
Some links in our Resources section are affiliate links. If you click and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Sponsored content is clearly marked "Sponsored."
Data accuracy
All layoff entries are sourced from publicly reported news. We link to the original source for every verified entry. User-submitted entries are marked "Pending" until confirmed against a credible source. If you believe any data is incorrect, please use the "Report incorrect data" link.
Your rights
You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing contact@ailayofftracker.org. If you are in the EU or UK, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access and correct your data.
Cookies
This site stores only your petition signature count locally in your browser using localStorage. No tracking cookies are used.
Contact
For any privacy questions: contact@ailayofftracker.org
